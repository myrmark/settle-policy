Privacy Policy – Settle: Split the Bill Easily Last updated: March 13, 2026 Overview Settle is an iOS app for splitting shared expenses between people. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is used and how. Data You Enter All data you enter in the app — trips, people, payments, and amounts — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never sent to our servers, because we have no servers. Exchange Rates When you select a foreign currency for a payment, the app fetches the current exchange rate from frankfurter.app, a free public API powered by European Central Bank data. Only the currency codes (e.g. "USD", "EUR") are sent in this request — no personal information is transmitted. Fetched rates are cached on your device for offline use. Advertising This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information to serve ads, including: Advertising identifiers (IDFA) Device information Ad interaction data This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your iPhone's Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking. Data Sharing We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party, except as described above for Google AdMob. Children This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.
